# Air quality analysis
This is a team project about an end-to-end pipeline analyzing air quality of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, including collecting data through OpenWeather API, clean data, make analysis and apply machine learning for predicting Air Quality Score (AQI)

Work management by Notion: [Notion](https://www.notion.so/36707dd2fd9b4ae39e69e7a48786e359?v=efb5c1e8fddd44bd80fdad5533db2021&pvs=4)

Insight/Result:
  - Air quality in Ho Chi Minh City during the two years from 2021 to 2023 was generally poor, with consistently high levels of dust and pollutants, and a tendency to increase year by year -> To improve air pollution, proactive measures from authorities and the cooperation of the public are needed. People should also limit their time outdoors during months with high levels of pollutants, as this can negatively impact their health.
  - Four model prototypes are proposed to predict air quality index with an accuracy of approximately 80%.

Potential development:
  - Learn more about the environmental field to gain deeper insights into the data.
  - Finetune and improve the predictive model to achieve more accurate results (around 90%).
